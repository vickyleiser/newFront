ABOUT US

At Pulmo.AI, we are committed to improving the field of respiratory care with the help of modern technology. Our mission is to make diagnosing and managing respiratory diseases easier by using artificial intelligence (AI) and detailed spirometry data analysis. Our project aims to help in the early detection of conditions like COPD, asthma, and pulmonary fibrosis by simplifying the process of analyzing spirometry data.


We work to make the diagnostic process quicker and more accurate, saving valuable time for healthcare professionals. Our easy-to-use web platform allows medical staff not only to view diagnostic results but also to enter important patient information, such as name, age, weight, and other relevant details. This information helps in making more accurate diagnoses and understanding the patient's condition better.


Pulmo.AI is dedicated to continuously improving our platform to meet the needs of healthcare providers. We aim to give doctors and clinicians effective tools that can help them provide better care. Join us in our journey to make respiratory healthcare better and improve the lives of patients everywhere.
